What is MediAI about?

is a smart platform that uses artificial intelligence to analyze health information provided by users, such as blood pressure and other data. Based on this analysis, it recommends appropriate medications and delivers them directly from the factory to the user. The goal is to simplify the treatment process and improve access to medical services.

What kind of health data does MediAI require?

MediAI requires health metrics such as blood pressure, heart rate, and other essential health parameters to offer personalized medical solutions.

How does MediAI work?

Our AI system uses the user's blood pressure, medical history, and other relevant factors to algorithmically suggest the right medication. This machine learning system uses medical advances to create a safe and intelligent model.
